All About us

Red Oil industries is a respected company in Lagos,known for its commitment to sustainability and community development. They prioritize ethical sourcing practices, ensuring fair wages for farmers and minimizing environmental impact.

Okomu banga oil is a good source of vitamin A, E, and K, as well as antioxidants. These Nutrients contribute to overall health and well-being.

It has high smoke point, making it ideal for high-heat cooking methods like frying and sauteing without producing harmful compounds.

It's widely used in West African cuisine, particularly in Nigeria, for cooking a variety of dishes, including soups, stews, and sauces.

Okomu Banga Oil imparts a rich, slightly nutty flavor to dishes, enhancing the taste of soups, stews, and other culinary creations.

Some studies suggest that banga oil may have potential health benefits, such as reducing inflammation and improving heart health.

Its a natural product, free from artificial additives and preservatives, making it s preferred choice for health-conscious individual.
